# Plan: Scroll Convergence Animation for Hero Cards

## Problem Statement

The user wants the 8 Pokemon cards from the hero section (4 on left, 4 on right) to animate and converge to the center of the screen as the user scrolls down. The cards should straighten their rotation and stack on top of each other. Marketing text panels should appear on the left and right sides during this scroll animation.

## Current State

- `HeroParallax` component renders 8 cards (4 left, 4 right) with mouse parallax
- Cards have scattered positions with rotations (-25°, 18°, -12°, 22° on left; 20°, -15°, 12°, -20° on right)
- The scroll convergence component was added as a separate section with different cards

## Desired Behavior

1. Hero section remains visible at top
2. As user scrolls down, the 8 cards animate from their scattered positions toward center
3. Cards straighten rotation toward 0° as they converge
4. Cards stack in center with slight offset for depth
5. Left and right marketing panels fade in during scroll
6. Cards fade out or stay stacked when convergence completes

## Implementation Plan

### Step 1: Modify HeroParallax Component

- Add scroll progress tracking using `useScroll` from framer-motion
- Create scroll-linked transforms for each of the 8 cards
- Cards should interpolate from initial position to center stack position based on scroll progress
- Hide cards or fade them as they converge (0-50% scroll progress)

### Step 2: Add Marketing Panels

- Create left panel: "Stop Wasting Time" pain point content
- Create right panel: "Tournament Ready" value prop content
- Panels slide in from sides (left: -100px→0, right: 100px→0)
- Opacity fades in (0→1) during scroll 20-40%
- Panels fade out (1→0) after 60% scroll

### Step 3: Center Stack Positioning

- Calculate center positions for all 8 cards with slight offsets:
  - Left cards: x: -60 to -15, slight y offsets
  - Right cards: x: 15 to 60, slight y offsets
  - All cards: rotate toward 0°, scale to ~0.85

### Step 4: Scroll Container Setup

- Wrap hero in a tall container (150vh) to allow scroll space
- Use sticky positioning so cards animate while scrolling
- Reset cards when scrolling back up

## File Changes

- `components/landing/HeroParallax.tsx`:
  - Add `useScroll` hook
  - Add scroll-based transforms to ParallaxCard
  - Add marketing panel components
  - Wrap in scroll container
